Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026 · Findr (ABN sole trader) · Australia

1. Who We Are

Findr is operated by a sole trader based in Victoria, Australia. We operate under Australian Privacy Law including the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth). For privacy enquiries contact support@findr.com

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use Findr:

  • Account information: Your email address and encrypted password
  • Search data: The watchlists, keywords, and criteria you create
  • Usage data: Scan activity, match results, and notification interactions
  • Payment information: Processed securely via Stripe — we never store card details

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the Findr service
  • To send you match notifications and service updates
  • To process payments and manage your subscription
  • To improve the accuracy of our search and scoring system
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers hosted by Railway (USA). We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from misuse, loss, and unauthorised access. Passwords are encrypted using industry standard bcrypt hashing and are never stored in plain text.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Railway: Hosting provider
  • Stripe: Payment processing
  • Search API providers: To execute searches on your behalf (only keywords are shared, never personal details)
  • Law enforcement: Where required by Australian law

6. Cookies

Findr uses local browser storage (not cookies) to store your login session token. We do not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies.

7. Your Rights

Under Australian Privacy Law you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data

To exercise these rights contact support@findr.com

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account we will remove your personal information within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it by law.

9. Children's Privacy

Findr is available to users of all ages. If you are under 18, we encourage parental awareness of your use of the Service. We collect only the minimum information necessary to operate the Service.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance.

11. Contact

For privacy enquiries contact support@findr.com
